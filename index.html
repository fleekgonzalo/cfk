Cristina Kirchner

Cristina Fernández de Kirchner is an Argentine politician who served as the President of Argentina from 2007 to 2015. She was the first woman to be elected as President of Argentina.

During her presidency, Kirchner implemented several policies aimed at improving social welfare and reducing poverty. She also nationalized several industries including the country's largest oil company, YPF.

After her presidency, Kirchner became a Senator for the Province of Buenos Aires and later served as Vice President of Argentina under Alberto Fernández.

Despite her controversial legacy, Cristina Kirchner remains a prominent figure in Argentine politics.
